Frequently Asked Questions

How much does native forest restoration cost?

Costs vary by site conditions, species selection, and scale. Many projects qualify for government subsidies that can cover 50-80% of costs. We provide free initial assessments and detailed quotes.

How long does it take to see results?

Fast-growing natives like manuka and kanuka provide ground cover within 1-2 years. Erosion control benefits are immediate, while full forest establishment takes 10-15 years.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer maintenance packages including weed control, replacement planting, and monitoring for the first 3-5 years to ensure establishment success.

Can I still graze livestock in planted areas?

Trees need protection for the first few years, but silvopasture systems can incorporate native trees with grazing. We can design systems that work with your farming operation.
